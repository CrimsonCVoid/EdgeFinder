Understanding EV, arbitrage, and advanced betting concepts

Expected Value (EV)

Expected Value represents the average amount you can expect to win or lose per bet over the long term. Positive EV (+EV) bets are profitable over time.

EV% = (Fair Probability / Book Probability) - 1

Arbitrage

Arbitrage occurs when you can bet on all possible outcomes of an event across different sportsbooks and guarantee a profit regardless of the result.

Kelly Criterion

The Kelly Criterion helps determine optimal bet sizing to maximize long-term growth while managing risk.

Kelly% = (bp - q) / b
where b = net odds, p = win probability, q = lose probability
